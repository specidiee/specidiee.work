# Thumbnails
To add a thumbnail, simply add a `thumbnail` field to the frontmatter of your `.mdx` file:

```yaml
---
title: "My Post"
thumbnail: "/images/my-image.png"
---
```
Ensure the image exists in the `public/images/` folder.
